MISSION

Our mission is to drive innovation by seamlessly transitioning laboratory research into commercial applications, supported by state-of-the-art facilities, a team of experts, dedicated mentors, and robust industry-academia partnerships. We are committed to fostering an environment that encourages collaboration, creativity, and excellence, ultimately contributing to societal well-being and technological advancement.

VISION

The Centre for Nanobiotechnology at AIC Raise aspires to become a world-class technology development hub that harnesses the power of nanoscience and biotechnology to tackle critical societal challenges, advance fundamental scientific knowledge, and develop cutting-edge technologies and solutions.

VALUES

RAISE Centre for Nanobiotechnology values information sustainability, and societal impact. It empowers startups and researchers through mentorship and collaboration, aiming to develop real-world solutions with global relevance.
